Hello there, I'm Anderson Maia, a Brazilian freelance illustrator, specialized in characters and creatures design.

I've been a professional illustrator since 2016, working with RPG publishers from around the world.

Drawing and painting are my passions, as well as heavy metal, horror, fantasy and science fiction.

I'd love to use the huge library of cool ideas I collected over the years in my head on your books and games, so feel free to contact me anytime!
